Introducing LibreOffice and its main features.

LibreOffice (Figure 1) is a free and open source software. It is designed and built by a community of people around the globe. It's main goal is to provide a free and libre software to create different documents. Originally LibreOffice was OpenOffice, but after OpenOffice SunMicro was acquired by Oracle, some original creators of OpenOffice forked it and started a LibreOffice in 2010. Since then, there are around 80 million of users.
