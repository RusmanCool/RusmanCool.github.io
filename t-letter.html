Writing a letter in LibreOffice suite

Steps

You can write a letter to your parents or your friends or any other written document in LibreOffice Writer. For this you need to:

  1. Launch either the main LibreOffice window (Figure 1) and choose Writer Document (Figure 1).

    2. Or Launch Writer Document from the Program Menu (Figure 2)

  2. Start writing and editing your letter.

    3. In the main Writer window (Figure 3), you can choose the Formatting Style, Font style and size, text alignment, and other.
