After a successful installation

Pre-requisites

  • You should have successfully installed LibreOffice.

Steps

After LibreOffice has been successfully installed onto your Windows computer, you need to launch it. For this you have two options:

  • Launch LibreOffice suite itself and choose the desired program in it (Figure 1).
  • Or you can launch separately LibreOffice programs using their own shortcuts(Figure 2)

    • Thus, each time you need to create a new document in LibreOffice, you can choose either of the options. I will show you how in next pages.
